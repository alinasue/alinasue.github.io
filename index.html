Statement:

I received my bachelor's degree from Lewis and Clark College (Spring 2012) with a double major in Psychology and Foreign Languages (Russian and French).

I have since received my doctorate from UC Santa Cruz in Cognitive Psychology (Spring 2019). My research primarily focused on first impressions in the context of human-computer interaction. In my research, I examined the impact of visual and rhythmic expression on personality judgments of machine and human agents alike.
